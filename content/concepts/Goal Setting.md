# Goal Setting

The process of defining specific outcomes to work toward. Referenced across multiple notes as a foundation for planning and execution.

## In the context of 12 Week Year

The [[12 Week Year]] framework argues that annual goal setting is unreliable — the long horizon invites complacency. 12-week cycles make goals concrete and urgent.

Effective goals in this framework:
- Are tied to a longer-term [[Vision]] so short-term sprints compound toward something meaningful.
- Are broken down into weekly milestones immediately after being set.
- Are reviewed weekly to catch drift early.
