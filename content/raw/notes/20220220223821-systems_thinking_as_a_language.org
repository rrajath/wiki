#+title: Systems Thinking as a Language
#+filetags: :literature-note:

Languages we use for speaking and conversations are linear signifying "x causes y". This linearity causes one-way relationships. In the real world, systems and interdependencies inside of a system are much more complex where "x" and "y" have some kind of effect on each other. This causes circular relationships and feedback loops.

- Source: Systems Thinking Tools (p6)
