#+title: Career Capital Market
#+filetags: :literature-note:

There are two types of market:
1. *Winner take all*: there's only one type of [[id:BA2618D2-8EE2-4F58-BA7C-BE1B4B8FA18B][Career Capital]] available and lot of people are competing for it. For example, television writing has only one capital - writing good scripts.
2. *Auction*: there are different types of [[id:BA2618D2-8EE2-4F58-BA7C-BE1B4B8FA18B][Career Capital]] and each person can generate a unique collection.
