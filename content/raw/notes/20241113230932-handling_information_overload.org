#+title: Handling information overload
[2022-02-27 Sun 13:13]
Split your information intake into 3 categories:
1. Fixed number of books to read each month
2. Have only one obsession at a time (instead of getting overwhelmed by all the things you want to do)
3. Schedule time slots of frivolous things like watching YouTube, newsletters, etc.
