#+title: Contemplative Reading
#+filetags: :blog-post:literature-note:

Most reading is fast and consumptive. We tend to read a lot more than what we can retain. Contemplative reading is slow where you read a few pages on a topic and contemplate about it. One could take months to read contemplatively and get through dense books. This helps with both retention and deeper understanding.

Also, there is never enough time to read all the books in our to-read list.
