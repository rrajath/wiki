#+title: Progesterone
#+filetags: :floating-note:ivf:

* Responsible for:
** preparing the uterus for potential pregnancy by promoting endometrial thickening and vascularization
** supporting pregnancy by reducing uterine contractions and promoting gestation
** inhibiting lactation during pregnancy
** [[id:6F782B51-31B9-40E4-99B9-A9416F76EA69][Regulating the menstrual cycle]], particularly the [[id:5B745668-F3C6-43A5-9271-E05E68D4A305][Secretory Phase]]
** balancing the effects of estrogen on the [[id:4779E410-C7AC-4F6D-891F-F2F647A5D6E7][Endometrium]]
