#+title: Awareness
#+filetags: :floating-note:

I keep coming back to this topic of awareness and it applies to a lot more things than I thought it did. Awareness is something fundamental. It is a way to improve how we notice things that we would otherwise miss. To see patterns in chaos. To have a different perspective. To think the right thoughts at the right time that help shape our mental model of the world and make it fit better.

It's like being in this meta state where you observe yourself and see how you react/respond to things. It is to "be aware" of your thoughts and actions in real time.
