#+title: Reference Class Forecasting
#+filetags: :literature-note:

This is a concept where you estimate how long a task takes by taking an average of all the estimates of similar tasks in that class. That will be the [[id:F12A50C7-72EC-481A-8FB2-C161090CDF26][Anchor]] for your estimate. If you think there are any variables in your task that will significantly reduce or increase your estimate, that will be your [[id:E10EB97E-829A-49FB-BFAF-CEA09A97C010][Adjusting]].
