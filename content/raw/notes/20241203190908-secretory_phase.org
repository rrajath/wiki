#+title: Secretory Phase
#+filetags: :ivf:floating-note:

* This is also known as the luteal phase, occurs during second half of the menstrual cycle
* The purpose of this phase is to prepare the uterine lining for potential implantation of the fertilized egg
* Begins after ovulation and lasts until the start of the next menstrual period (typically days 14-28 in a 28-day cycle)
* Endometrial changes:
** [[id:4779E410-C7AC-4F6D-891F-F2F647A5D6E7][Endometrium]] continues to mature and becomes more receptive to implantation
** increased blood flow to the uterus and promotion of uterine secretions
