#+title: Levels of Understanding in Systems Thinking
#+filetags: :literature-note:

- *events* (reactive): what is the fastest way to react to this event now?
- *patterns of events* (proactive): what kinds of trends or patterns of events seem to be recurring?
- *systemic structure* (creative): what are the mental organizational structures that create the patterns?
- *shared vision* (generative): what are the stated/unstated visions that generate the structures?

- Source: Systems Thinking (p9)
