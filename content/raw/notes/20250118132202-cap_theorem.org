#+title: CAP Theorem

In a distributed system, it is impossible to achieve more than two of the following three guarantees:
- [[id:2B17D96B-6E66-4690-8FBE-3F54C264270D][Consistency]]
- [[id:6EE33772-83EE-4051-BDF2-3AAB3F36165A][Availability]]
- [[id:1823035B-2731-4916-8B19-BEC2E044E13D][Partition Tolerance]]

Systems are generally classified based on which of the CAP attributes they support: CP, AP or CA, and the third attribute is sacrificed.
