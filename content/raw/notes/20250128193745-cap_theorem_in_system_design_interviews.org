#+title: CAP Theorem in System Design Interviews

In a system design interview, while gathering requirements, you'd gather functional and non-functional requirements. When it comes to non-functional requirements, you should start with the [[id:0858A664-2994-458D-812E-10AF43287EA3][CAP Theorem]].

The [[id:1823035B-2731-4916-8B19-BEC2E044E13D][Partition Tolerance in Distributed Systems]] is a must. So, it comes down to choosing between [[id:2B17D96B-6E66-4690-8FBE-3F54C264270D][Consistency]] and [[id:6EE33772-83EE-4051-BDF2-3AAB3F36165A][Availability]]. We can't have both. So, it's about trade offs.
