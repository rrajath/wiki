#+title: Accept reality as it is

This is often repeated in many contexts and scenarios. But we either miss the point or we don't remember it when it is most required. We get caught up in whatever is happening to us and we get so self-involved that we forget that the universe, or the reality, does not care about us. It just exists. It doesn't care about our opinion and it doesn't care how we feel. We talk about things being unfair or unjust, but at the end of the day, that's just reality. It's a mix of a bunch of cause and effects with a bit of what-we-think-is-randomness thrown in between.
