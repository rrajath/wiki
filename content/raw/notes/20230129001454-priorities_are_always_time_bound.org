#+title: Priorities are always time-bound
#+filetags: :permanent-note:

Any task you take, if you assign a priority to it, it is time-bound. In fact, every single task is time-bound. Something that’s higher priority literally means it must be done sooner rather than later. This seems obvious, but when put into these words, you’ll start thinking about tasks and priorities a little differently.
