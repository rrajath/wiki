#+title: Career Capital
#+filetags: :literature-note:

Career Capital is a skill you have or build to be successful in a career. For example, in order to be successful in television writing, you must be good at screen writing. This goes hand in hand with what kind of [[id:F4101EF9-70CB-4C67-AEA2-A76556E279E4][Career Capital Market]] you want to see yourself in.
