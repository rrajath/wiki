#+title: Vision

[2022-02-05 Sat 00:27]
Vision is supposed to be long term. It shouldn't include anything that can be achieved in the next couple of years. It need not be a specific endpoint. It can be a way of living that I have to carry out every moment. Here are some to start with, and definitely need adjustments:
+ live with integrity/sincerity/courage: own up to things I'm responsible for. Be brave in expressing opinions. Don't go with the herd. Search for truth.
+ Always be learning: be curious all the time and learn new things. And apply new things that you've learned.
+ Stick to fundamentals and draw your own conclusions: reason from first principles to know what to trust and what not to trust, don't go by what others say and just repeat stuff.
+ Be confident and believe in yourself: higher difference in thinking you can do something vs knowing you can. The latter is valued more.
