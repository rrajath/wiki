#+title: Proliferative Phase
#+filetags: :floating-note:ivf:

* This is also known as follicular phase - occurs during the first half of the menstrual cycle.
** it usually begins after menstruation ends, around days 4-7 and continues until ovulation (approx. day-14 or 28-day cycle)
** primarily driven be rising estrogen levels produced by developing ovarian follicles.
** [[id:4779E410-C7AC-4F6D-891F-F2F647A5D6E7][Endometrium]] changes:
*** endometrium thickens and regenerates after menstruation
*** glands in the endometrium elongate and become more curved
** purpose: to prepare the uterine lining for potential implantations of a fertilized egg
