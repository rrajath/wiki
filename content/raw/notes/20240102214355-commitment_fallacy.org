#+title: Commitment Fallacy
#+filetags: :literature-note:

This is the fallacy where people rush to commit to things even when they sometimes think that it’s not the wise thing to do. It may be because they were asked to do something by their superiors, or they don’t want to be the blocker. But ultimately, they tend to [[id:e590314a-2c78-4d61-ac82-6c7241417bf2][Think Fast, Act Slow]] making the project both delayed and complete with low quality, if they even complete at all.
